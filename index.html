project brief





Project utilizing clothing and accessories to identify oneself with the state of being lost and mistranslated. Apparel, branding, and jewelry designed to have individuals identify themselves with the state of being “ lost in translation. ” Project allowed an extensive understanding of production processes and communication with manufacturers.
The box mark U+ 25AF stands for non-representable and missing characters in text, and inspires the logo. Clothing such as bandana, shirt(L and M sizes), buckethats, and socks(L and M) were designed and produced with various versions. Newspaper print in broadsheet size documented modelshoots and names & narratives of models in relation to the theme of aliennation.


2019 – 2020
