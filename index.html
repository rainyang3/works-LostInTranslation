project brief

Project utilizing clothing and accessories to identify oneself with the state of being lost and mistranslated. Apparel, branding, and jewelry designed to have individuals identify themselves with the state of being “ lost in translation. ” Project allowed an extensive understanding of production processes and communication with manufacturers.

jewelry

Pendants, ring, bracelet and clothing pins made for the jewelry collection, all set in sterling silver. Three verisons of clothing tape made for user customization.
